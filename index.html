Biography

English was not my first language. I grew up to Korean immigrants and I am fully aware of my cultural identity woven between two distinct cultures. Food was my first language. I love to cook. I love playing sports. I love being outside. I love trying new things and experiences. Most importantly I love to learn and tinker.

I am trying to stay positive while drinking from a fire hose. I work full-time and trade sleep for coding. I am a full-time dad to a 18 month old son. My dog has forgotten who I am because I talk to Victor, my rubber ducky.


Idiosyncrasies: I think in Korean before I speak English. Now I am learning to code - what could possibly go wrong?
